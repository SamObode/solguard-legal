Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

SolGuard ("we", "our", "us") operates the SolGuard mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. We use Supabase for authentication and data storage.

Usage Data: We record which token addresses you scan and when, in order to enforce free tier limits and improve our service. We also store tokens you add to your watchlist.

Device Information: If you opt in to push notifications, we store your device push token to send you alerts.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide and maintain the SolGuard service, including token risk analysis, watchlist functionality, and account management. Enforce usage limits on the free tier. Send push notifications if you opt in. Improve and develop new features.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted database storage and Row Level Security ensuring you can only access your own data. Authentication tokens are stored on your device using encrypted secure storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Supabase: Authentication and database hosting. Helius: Solana blockchain data for token analysis. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your watchlist items directly in the app. For account deletion or data export requests, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

SolGuard is not intended for anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Disclaimer

SolGuard provides risk signals and analysis for informational purposes only. Nothing in this app constitutes financial advice. Always do your own research before making investment decisions.
